Description

The Filet-O-Fish is a fish sandwich sold by international fast food restaurant chain McDonald's. It consists of a breaded fish fillet, tartar sauce, and a slice of cheese, served on a steamed bun.

Ingredients

  • 1 breaded fish fillet (e.g., Alaskan Pollock)
  • 1 slice of cheese (e.g., American cheese)
  • Tartar sauce
  • 1 steamed bun
  • Lettuce (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare the fish fillet by breading it with a mixture of flour, breadcrumbs, and seasonings.
  2. Fry or bake the breaded fish fillet until golden brown and cooked through.
  3. Steam the bun until soft and warm.
  4. Spread tartar sauce on the bottom half of the steamed bun.
  5. Place the cooked fish fillet on top of the tartar sauce.
  6. Add a slice of cheese on top of the fish fillet.
  7. If desired, add lettuce for extra crunch.
  8. Cover with the top half of the steamed bun.
  9. Serve and enjoy your homemade Filet-O-Fish!
