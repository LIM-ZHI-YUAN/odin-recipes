Description

The Big Mac is a hamburger sold by international fast food restaurant chain McDonald's. It is one of the company's flagship products and is known for its distinctive taste and structure.

Ingredients

  • 2 sesame seed buns
  • 1 beef patty
  • 1 slice of cheese
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Pickles
  • Onions
  • Special sauce (mayonnaise, ketchup, mustard, relish)

Steps

  1. Toast the sesame seed buns.
  2. Cook the beef patty on a grill or stovetop until fully cooked.
  3. Assemble the burger by placing the bottom bun on a plate.
  4. Add the beef patty on top of the bottom bun.
  5. Place a slice of cheese on the patty.
  6. Add shredded lettuce, pickles, and onions on top of the cheese.
  7. Spread the special sauce on the top bun.
  8. Place the top bun on the assembled ingredients.
  9. Serve and enjoy your homemade Big Mac!
